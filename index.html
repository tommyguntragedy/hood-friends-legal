HoodFriends Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how HoodFriends (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use the HoodFriends mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, display name, and profile photo URL from Google.
  • Username. An optional username you choose within the App.
  • Trade data. Trade information that you upload by selecting or pasting a screenshot. The screenshot itself is processed on your device using Apple’s Vision framework; we never store or transmit the original image. Only the parsed text data (such as ticker symbol, strike, expiration, and premium) is saved to your account.

Information collected automatically

  • Activity metadata. Timestamps of your uploads and aggregate statistics derived from your uploaded trades (e.g., prior-week premium received, settled P&L).
  • Friendship records. Records of friend requests sent, received, and accepted between users of the App.
  • Reactions. Emoji reactions you place on trades visible to you.

We do not collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or location data. The App does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To display your trades and aggregate statistics to friends you have accepted.
  • To allow other users to find you and send you friend requests via email or username.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App.

How We Share Your Information

  • With friends you accept. Your username, display name, trade history, and prior-week statistics are visible to users with whom you have a confirmed friendship in the App.
  • Service providers. We use Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore) to authenticate users and store the data described above. Firebase processes data on our behalf under its own privacy and security commitments. See firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
  • Legal. We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights or the safety of others.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains in our systems for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us at the address below. We will delete your authentication records, profile, trade history, friendship records, and reactions within 30 days of a verified request.

Trade data also persists locally on your device. Uninstalling the App removes the local copy.

Children’s Privacy

HoodFriends is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and to withdraw consent for processing. Email us at the address below to exercise any of these rights.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Google Firebase to protect your data in transit (TLS) and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Effective Date” above. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
